The more, the better. The equation is straightforward - the more time you can invest, the more content you can create. We use this content for your marketing, which leads to higher revenue. You should plan to spend at least 1-2 hours per day producing content. This allows us to optimally support you in becoming successful on OnlyFans.

How long before I see results?

With the Fixed Salary option, you’ll receive your first payment exactly seven days after starting.

With a Revenue Share model, it depends on whether you already have a social media account or if you’re starting from scratch.

  • If you already have an account with content, you may start seeing earnings in the first few days.

  • If you’re starting fresh, it typically takes around two weeks before you see your first income.

What's the contract duration?

None! At JP Management, there's no minimum contract term. You can leave anytime you want. We believe in collaboration based on conviction, not obligation!

Which social media platforms will I be active on?

We are mostly active on TikTok, Instagram, and Threads, since they provide the biggest reach. In addition, we sometimes use Reddit and Twitter to share content.

Will I remain anonymous in my home country?

Absolutely! We primarily target the US market and have developed special strategies to ensure you remain undiscovered in your home country. Your privacy is our highest priority. We'll handle the rest!
